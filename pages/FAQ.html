Frequently asked questions

A lot of users ask a lot of questions, here are a few most commonly asked questions with answers:

How to join the guild in-game?

In order to join the guild in-game, we need to verify that you meet our requirements before we can accept your join request.
For that, open a ticket in the Discord server.

What if I don't meet the requirements?

Unfortunately, if you don't meet our requirements you can't join the guild in-game.
But you can join our Discord server and ask for advice and have fun!

How to become a staff member?

In order to become a staff member, you will have to fill out this form: apply.nlh.wtf.
We will review it and decide if you are suitable for a staff position.
